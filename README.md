# CSSharp Wallhack and Invisible plugin

A CSSharp plugin which can enable server side wallhack for player and make players invisible

Based on content by dima_wallhacks

## Features 
- Wallhack | give players wallhack on the server side
- Invisible players | makes players and their weapons invisible to other players unless they make sound
- Self Damage | Makes players take damage when they miss a shot

## Wallhack

Gives a player a wallhack like the spectator glow

`!wh <Player|@ct|@t>`

`!wallhack <Player|@ct|@t>`

![Wallhack-demo](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f464c0ba-db46-4e7a-ae5c-ff46a1e6fe7d)

### Dynamic colour

Dynamic colour changes the colour of the wallhack based on the players health. It can be enable and disabled in the config

![Dynamic-colour-demo](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c601cbfb-7bf2-4aab-a7e3-c1e2aa21e1cd)

## Invisible

Makes a player invisible to other players unless they make noise

`!invis <Player|@ct|@t>`

`!invisible <Player|@ct|@t>`

![Invis-demo](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/fa08a671-3ef6-4dce-a9e9-3b74bdf1bf0f)

## Self Damage 

When enable for a player self damage means that a player will take damage every time they shoot and miss the enemy 

All damage values can be edited in the config

`!sdamage <Player|@ct|@t>`

`!selfdamage <Player|@ct|@t>`

![self-damage-demo](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d564c694-dafc-44bc-8361-a0a4a7b6ea93)
